Pasta Carbonara

The "Pasta Carbonara" is a typical Italian plate liked all around the world during decades.

Its recipe is simple and quick to make and we'll explain it below!


Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta

Steps
  1. Put the pasta on boiled water
  2. Chop the pancetta and mix it with cheese
  3. Beat the eggs
  4. Mix all together until smoothy and creamy!
